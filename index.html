📅 Slot Booking Instructions

  • Each slot is 2 hours long with a 30-minute grace period
  • Maximum 4 people allowed per single QR code / booking
  • Select your preferred date and available time slot
  • Fill in visitor name, contact number and number of people
  • Click "Confirm & Generate QR" to get your QR code
  • Download or screenshot the QR for offline use

📱 QR Code Usage

  • Show QR at the entry gate – scanner will verify your booking
  • One QR covers all people in your booking group
  • QR is valid from your slot start time until exit scan
  • QR cannot be reused once exit is scanned
  • Keep phone brightness high for smooth scanning
  • Battery backup (power bank) recommended

⚠️ Rules & Penalties

  • Maximum stay: 2 hours + 30-minute grace period
  • Overstay beyond grace period: ₹100 penalty notification sent
  • Mobile photography permitted only in designated zones
  • Maintain decorum and silence in sanctum area
  • No food or beverages inside the main temple hall
  • Footwear must be removed at designated areas
  • Dress code strictly followed – traditional attire preferred

🗺️ Understanding the Heatmap

The heatmap shows real-time crowd distribution across temple zones:

  • Green (Low): Zone is comfortable, easy movement
  • Yellow (Medium): Moderate crowd, slight queuing expected
  • Red (High): Overcrowded – staff will manage entry

Data updates every 5 seconds from QR scan entries.

🔔 Notification Guide

  • Entry Confirmation: Sent after QR scan at gate
  • Darshan Time: Real-time expected wait estimate
  • 10-min Warning: Alert before slot expires
  • Exit Reminder: Prompt to scan exit QR
  • Penalty Alert: If overstay detected
